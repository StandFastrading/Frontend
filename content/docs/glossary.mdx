# Glossary

## Trading terms

**P&L** — Profit and Loss. Your realized gain or loss over a period.

**Win rate** — Percentage of trades that closed profitably. High win rate isn't enough on its own — small wins and big losses can still bleed an account.

**R-multiple** — Profit/loss measured in units of risk. A trade that risked $100 and made $300 is 3R. Tracking R-multiples normalizes across position sizes.

**Drawdown** — The peak-to-trough decline in your equity curve. Max drawdown matters more than max gain for staying in the game.

**Setup** — A specific, repeatable pattern you trade. E.g. "opening range breakout."

## Standfast terms

**Session** — A discrete trading period (usually a day) with a defined start and end. The basic unit of journaling.

**Rule** — A pre-committed constraint on your trading. Standfast tracks adherence over time.

**Rule violation** — When you act against a rule you set. Logged automatically when detected.

**Intervention** — An opt-in real-time nudge from Standfast when you're drifting from your plan.
