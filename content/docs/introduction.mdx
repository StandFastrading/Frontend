# Introduction

**Standfast** is a trading journal built around one idea: most blown-up accounts aren't a strategy problem, they're an emotional control problem. Standfast helps you notice when you're trading from fear, FOMO, revenge, or boredom — and pull yourself back to your plan before it costs you.

## Who it's for

Active day traders who:

- Already have a strategy with a defined edge
- Have rules they *intend* to follow (max losses, stop times, risk limits)
- Find themselves breaking those rules in the moment

If you're still discovering your edge, journaling-first tools like this can help — but a sandbox or backtester might be a better starting point.

## How it works

- **Sessions** — start a session before you trade, end it after. Tag your mental state.
- **Journal** — quick reflections at session end. Optional structured prompts.
- **Trades** — log entries/exits, ideally automated from your broker over time.
- **Rules** — define your guardrails. Standfast checks against them in real time.
- **Interventions** — opt-in nudges when you cross a rule, drift from your plan, or show patterns linked to your worst sessions.
