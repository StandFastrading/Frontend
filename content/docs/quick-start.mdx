# Quick start

A 5-minute guide to your first journaled trading session.

## 1. Sign up

Create an account at [/auth](/auth) and use the Sign Up tab. You'll be active immediately — no email confirmation while we're early.

## 2. Set your first rules

Head to your [dashboard](/dashboard) and define at least one trading rule. The classics:

- **Max daily loss** — stop trading at $X drawdown
- **Max trades per day** — circuit-breaker against overtrading
- **No-trade hours** — e.g. don't trade the first 5 minutes after open

## 3. Start a session

Before your first trade, click **Start session** on the dashboard. Tag your mental state — focused, distracted, anxious, whatever's honest.

## 4. Trade

Log trades manually for now. Broker integrations come later.

## 5. End the session

When you're done for the day, end the session. Standfast will prompt you for a short reflection — what worked, what didn't, did you follow your rules?

That reflection is the most valuable thing in this app. It's where patterns become visible over weeks.
