# Rules

Standfast's rule engine is the difference between a passive journal and an active coach. But rules only work if they're real constraints, not aspirations.

## What makes a good rule

A good Standfast rule is:

1. **Measurable** — can be evaluated without judgment. "Max 3 trades per day" is measurable. "Don't overtrade" is not.
2. **Time-bounded** — has a clear scope (per trade, per session, per week).
3. **Actionable in the moment** — you can stop, change behavior, and comply right now.

## Examples

- *Max 2% account risk per trade*
- *Stop trading after 2 consecutive losses*
- *No new positions after 3 PM*
- *No revenge trades within 10 minutes of a loss*
